April 25-26, 2022: NWIT Lightning Talks

People have been writing software for over seventy years, and studying it for nearly as long, but most programmers don't know what researchers have discovered, and most researchers aren't looking at the problems developers would most like solved.

We can do better. On Tuesday, April 25, and Wednesday, April 26, It Will Never Work in Theory is offering its third live event: a set of lightning talks from leading researchers on immediate, actionable results from their work.
